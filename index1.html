Relief Package

High Tech Farm Laboratory

They researched and studied extensively, reading about new techniques like water recycling and land rehabilitation. They also started paying closer attention to the ground water and the way it moved through their fields. And slowly but surely, they began to develop a new approach to farming that incorporated all these concepts into a single, cohesive package. At the heart of their new approach was a deep respect for the natural world and a commitment to working with it rather than against it. They learned how to stack different crops together to create a more diverse ecosystem, and they used cover crops to protect and nourish the soil during the off-season.
